Website Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Whitewater Rafting

This name represents a travel blog focused on eco-friendly travel tips, destinations, and experiences.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide information and resources for participants who are interested in sustainable and eco-friendly rafting options. It will feature blog posts, trip guides, and recommendations for pricing.

Scenarios

  • What are some eco-friendly rafting locations in Ghana?
  • How can I get best and affordable pricing for my family and friends?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #6d99da (sky blue) - used for headers, footer, and navigation bar

Secondary Color: #FFFFFF (White) - used for background and main text

Accent Color: #000000 (black) - used for paragrph, and subtitles

Typography

Header Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif - used for headings

Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - used for body text
